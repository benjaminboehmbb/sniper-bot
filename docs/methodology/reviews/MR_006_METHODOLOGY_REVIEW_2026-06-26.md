Maintain the current Scientific Derivation Methodology without modification.

The existing methodology successfully completed the derivation of SGF-011 while demonstrating scientific self-correction and maintaining full traceability.

No evidence currently justifies structural modification of the SDM.

Preserve SF-001 as reusable scientific knowledge.

## Recommendation 3

Continue evaluating MWH-007 and MWH-008.
