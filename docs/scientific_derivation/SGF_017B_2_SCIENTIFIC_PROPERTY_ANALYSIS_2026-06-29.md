# Definition of Scientific Property Analysis

Scientific Property Analysis is the systematic scientific evaluation of an identified Property Candidate.

Its purpose is to determine whether the identified Property Candidate:

- represents a scientifically meaningful characteristic;
- is scientifically necessary;
- is scientifically minimal;
- possesses an independent scientific responsibility;
- is not redundant with existing Scientific Core concepts.

Scientific Property Analysis therefore evaluates the scientific characteristics of a Property Candidate against the certified Scientific Property requirements rather than introducing new scientific concepts.

---

# Scientific Property Analysis Principles

Every identified Property Candidate shall satisfy the following principles.

## Principle 1

### Observation-Based

Scientific Property Analysis shall be based exclusively upon previously established scientific observations.

No Property Candidate shall be accepted solely because it appears intuitively useful.

---

## Principle 2

### Scientific Necessity

Every Property Candidate shall represent a scientifically necessary characteristic required for addressing the associated proposed Scientific Capability Gap.

A Property Candidate shall not be accepted merely because it improves scientific description or descriptive convenience.

---

## Principle 3

### Scientific Minimality

Every Property Candidate shall represent one minimal scientifically distinguishable characteristic.

If a candidate can be decomposed into simpler independent characteristics,

the original candidate shall not be accepted unchanged.

---

## Principle 4

### Scientific Independence

Every Property Candidate shall possess exactly one primary scientific responsibility.

Multiple independent scientific responsibilities shall not be combined within a single Scientific Property.

---

## Principle 5

### Scientific Non-Redundancy

Every Property Candidate shall contribute a scientific characteristic not already completely represented by the certified Scientific Core.

Redundant Property Candidates shall not be promoted to Scientific Properties.

---

## Principle 6

### Domain Independence

Every Scientific Property shall remain independent of:

- trading;
- SSI;
- software implementation;
- engineering optimisation;
- application-specific domains.

---

## Principle 7

### Scientific Traceability

Every scientific conclusion reached during Property Analysis shall remain directly traceable to:

- previous scientific observations;
- the associated proposed Scientific Capability Gap;
- the corresponding Property Candidate.
