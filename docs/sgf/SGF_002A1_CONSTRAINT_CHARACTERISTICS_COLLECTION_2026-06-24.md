# Purpose

Collect observable characteristics of statements commonly interpreted as constraints.

No interpretation, classification or derivation shall be performed during this phase.

---

# Scientific Question

Which observable characteristics are consistently present in statements commonly interpreted as constraints?

---

# Inputs

Accepted inputs:

- Candidate constraint-like statements
- Validated SGF-000
- Validated SGF-001

Forbidden inputs:

- constraint definitions
- scientific classifications
- implementation considerations
- architectural assumptions
- preferred solutions

---

# Observation Rules

Each characteristic shall satisfy all of the following:

- directly observable
- independent of interpretation
- reproducible
- formulation-independent where possible
