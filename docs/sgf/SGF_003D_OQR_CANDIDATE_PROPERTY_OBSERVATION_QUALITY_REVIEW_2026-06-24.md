# Purpose

Evaluate whether each collected candidate property observation satisfies the requirements for scientific observation before characteristics analysis or property assessment is performed.

No Candidate Property shall be recognized and no Necessary Property shall be derived during this review.

---

# Scientific Question

Are the collected candidate property observations sufficiently objective to justify further scientific analysis?

---

# Observation Quality Criteria

Every observation shall be evaluated using:

- direct observability,
- interpretation independence,
- observation minimality,
- reproducibility.

---

Review:

The phrase "independently of any particular implementation" may introduce theoretical interpretation about implementation independence.

Revised Observation:

Decision:
PASS WITH REVISION

Reviewer Reasoning:

The revised formulation preserves the observable phenomenon while reducing theoretical content.
