# Relationship Quality Review (RQR)

Purpose:

Evaluate whether proposed relationships between observations are justified before they become accepted analysis results.

The review evaluates relationships rather than individual observations.

---

## RQR-1 — Evidence Support

Question:

Is the proposed relationship directly supported by the accepted observations?

Decision:

PASS / FAIL

---

## RQR-2 — Interpretation Independence

Question:

Does the relationship avoid introducing additional interpretation beyond the accepted observations?

Decision:

PASS / FAIL

---

## RQR-3 — Relationship Minimality

Question:

Is this the weakest relationship that is fully justified by the available observations?

Decision:

PASS / FAIL

Reviewer Principle:

Reduce analytical assumptions until further reduction would remove the relationship itself.

---

## RQR-4 — Reproducibility

Question:

Would independent reviewers identify the same relationship using the same accepted observations?

Decision:

PASS / FAIL
