# Purpose

Evaluate whether the proposed relationships identified during Candidate Property Characteristics Analysis are sufficiently supported by the accepted observations before any Candidate Property Necessity Test is performed.

No Candidate Property shall be recognized during this review.

---

# Scientific Question

Are the proposed relationships sufficiently justified to support subsequent Candidate Property Necessity Assessment?

---

# Relationship Quality Criteria

Every proposed relationship shall satisfy:

- evidence support,
- interpretation independence,
- relationship minimality,
- reproducibility.
