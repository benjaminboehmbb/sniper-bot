# Purpose

Evaluate whether each collected observation satisfies the requirements for a scientific observation before any interpretation or analysis is performed.

---

# Scientific Question

Are the collected observations sufficiently objective to justify further scientific analysis?

---

# Observation Quality Criteria

Every observation shall be:

- directly observable
- interpretation-independent
- reproducible
- formulation-independent where practical
- free of implicit scientific conclusions
- free of implementation assumptions
